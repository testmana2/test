sbsdiff(a, b, linenumberwidth = 4)

a
first sequence of lines (list of strings)
b
second sequence of lines (list of strings)
linenumberwidth

Returns:
a generator yielding tuples of differences. The tuple is composed of strings as follows. <ul> <li>opcode -- one of e, d, i, r for equal, delete, insert, replace</li> <li>lineno a -- linenumber of sequence a</li> <li>line a -- line of sequence a</li> <li>lineno b -- linenumber of sequence b</li> <li>line b -- line of sequence b</li> </ul>
